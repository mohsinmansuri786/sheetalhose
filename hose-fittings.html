Hose Fittings

Sheetal Hose Industries are manufactures a wide range of Hydraulic Hose End Fittings, from low to ultra high pressure range and compatible to high temperature, aggressive fluids in single/double wire braided and multi-spiral hoses. Our components conform to SAE, ISO, DIN/EN and other international standards. Hydraulic Fittings are designed for mounting on to hydraulic hose. The fitting must be compatible to the hose in order to withstand the rigors encountered in extreme working conditions.
